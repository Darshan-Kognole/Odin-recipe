Pizza

Description

A homemade pizza with a crispy crust, savory sauce, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a cozy dinner or family gathering.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs (oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  5. Add your favorite toppings like pepperoni and vegetables.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
