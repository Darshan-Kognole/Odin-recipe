Salad

Description

A refreshing and healthy salad made with fresh vegetables, a light dressing, and optional protein for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and carrots.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss well to combine and serve immediately.
