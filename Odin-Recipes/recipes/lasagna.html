Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian lasagna is a hearty dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and perfectly cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Spices (oregano, basil, garlic)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce and spices.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layering until all ingredients are used, ending with cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving.
